What is Data Privacy?

Data Privacy is the principle that a person should be able to control how their personal data is used and collected.
This site provides a basic understanding of how to protect your data from sites and malicious malware,
for a more in depth understanding, take the Cybersecurity and Privacy course on Coursera provided by Microsoft.

the majority of web pages and organisations collect your data and track your activity to tailor their sites and provide targeted ads. This is not a problem unless the site doesn't protect its user's data correctly and a hacker gets ahold of sensitive information, this is known as a 'data breach' and allows hackers to steal information that can be used for finantial fraud, idenity theft, or to resell it to other hackers.
